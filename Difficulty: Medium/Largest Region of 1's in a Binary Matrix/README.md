<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/length-of-largest-region-of-1s-1587115620/1">Largest Region of 1's in a Binary Matrix</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Given a grid of dimensions nxm containing 0's and 1's. A region of 1's is a group of 1's where two 1s can be adjacent to each other in any of the 8 directions ( 2 horizontal, 2 vertical and 4 diagonal).&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples :</strong></span></p>

<strong>Explanation: <br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/927581/Web/Other/blobid1_1777631747.png" width="127" height="110"><br></strong>The largest region has five 1s </span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>grid[][] = [[0,1]]
<strong>Output: </strong>1
<strong>Explanation: </strong>The largest region of 1's is <strong>1</strong>.</span>
</pre>
